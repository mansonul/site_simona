Who We Are

We help people and organizations grow strategically, sustainably, and with purpose.

Growth that starts with People.

At the core of our work is a simple truth: growth happens when people feel seen, understood, and empowered. That’s why we don’t offer quick fixes or generic solutions. We offer clarity, connection, and actionable tools that drive meaningful change—from insight to implementation.

I’m Simona Istrate, founder and lead strategist. Over the years, I’ve guided leaders, teams, and individuals through change, complexity, and transformation in companies such as Banca Transilvania, Honeywell Garrett, Unicredit, Alstom, and Alpha Bank, among other leading organizations.

What I’ve learned is this: true growth doesn’t come from being told what to do. It comes from recognizing your own strength and taking full ownership of your direction.

We partner with businesses and people who are ready for real progress. No fluff. No formulas. Just smart, human-centered strategies that stick.

Hello, I’m Simona.

I’ve lived a full life, raising a child, experiencing love, facing loss, and rebuilding after major life changes. These moments shaped me and taught me lessons no book ever could. For over 20 years, I’ve led complex projects with trusted collaborators, supporting growth through thoughtful, personalized approaches always delivering extraordinary results that exceed both my own and my clients’ expectations. I’ve never stopped learning, continuously exploring new methods and blending the most effective ones into a practical, human-centered way of working. I’m a realist with a background in math, physics, and economics. But what truly drives me is understanding people and their stories. That’s why I’ve been studying psychology for more than 30 years. My mission is simple: to help people grow, feel seen, and feel supported, one meaningful step at a time.
